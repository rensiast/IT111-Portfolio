SIGNIFICANCE OF GOOD HEALTH AND WELL BEING

Good health and well-being are essential for living a fulfilling and productive life. When we take care of our physical and mental health, we have more energy and focus to pursue our goals and enjoy daily activities. It also helps us build stronger relationships because we’re able to support others and be present for the people we care about. Prioritizing good health not only improves our quality of life but also ensures we can contribute positively to society.

Does everyone have access to healthcare?

In 2017, only around one third to half of the global population was covered by essential health services. If current trends continue, only 39 per cent to 63 per cent of the global population will be covered by essential health services by 2030.

The COVID-19 crisis has disrupted essential health services around the world. Some services have been suspended to free up resources for COVID-19 patients and to reduce the risk of transmission. If universal health coverage is to become a reality by 2030, growth in the provision and use of essential health services must greatly accelerate.

What can we do to help?

You can start by promoting and protecting your own health and the health of those around you, by making well-informed choices, practicing safe sex and vaccinating your children. You can raise awareness in your community about the importance of good health, healthy lifestyles as well as people’s right to quality health care services, especially for the most vulnerable such as women and children.

You can also hold your government, local leaders and other decisionmakers accountable to their commitments to improve people’s access to health and health care.
